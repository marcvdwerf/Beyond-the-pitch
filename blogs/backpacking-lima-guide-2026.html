Top Things to Do in Lima for Backpackers (2025 Guide)

Discover authentic Peru: from Miraflores surfing and Barranco culture to the intense Alianza Lima football experience.

Lima is the beating heart of Peru. For backpackers traveling through South America, it offers a raw mix of modern coastlines and deep-rooted traditions.

Why Backpacking Lima is a Must in 2025

While many head straight to Cusco, Lima offers the most authentic cultural experience in Peru. It's a city of contrasts where you can find world-class street food and vibrant neighborhoods like Barranco and Miraflores at a fraction of Western prices.

1. The Alianza Lima Football Matchday Experience

If you want to understand Peruvian passion, you need to visit Estadio Alejandro Villanueva. Watching Alianza Lima is more than a game; it's a cultural immersion. It is widely considered the most intense football experience in South America for travelers.

"The Alianza Lima match is the most authentic local experience you can have in the capital. It's pure energy, drums, and soul."

⚽ How to book the Alianza Lima Experience

Don't risk buying fake tickets outside. The Beyond the Pitch guided tour includes safe transport, official tickets, and a local guide to explain the chants and history. Perfect for solo travelers.

2. Cultural Exploration in Barranco

Barranco is the bohemian soul of the city. Known for its street art and the famous 'Bridge of Sighs', it's the best place for backpackers to find live music and local craft beer.

3. Adventure & Surfing in Miraflores

The Costa Verde cliffs offer incredible paragliding and budget-friendly surf lessons. It’s one of the few capitals in the world where you can catch waves right in the city center.

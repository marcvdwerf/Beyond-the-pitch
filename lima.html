About Lima

With a population of around ten million people, Lima stands as South America's second-largest metropolis. It is a vibrant mosaic of cultures, where the football club Alianza Lima holds a special place - a proud emblem of the city's spirit and its people's identity.

Why travel with us

With our program, you'll experience an Alianza Lima matchday just like a local fan; in addition to that, you've got the opportunity to step off the mainstream tourist path and discover Lima through the eyes of those who live it - all while being guided by a passionate local.

Sample experience

Morning - Neighborhood Discovery

Explore the La Victoria neighborhood, home to Alianza Lima's stadium, with a local guide who shares stories of the club's history and its deep connection to the community.

Afternoon - Pre-Match Atmosphere

Experience the pre-match buzz at local cantinas, enjoy authentic Peruvian street food, and meet passionate fans as they prepare for kickoff.

Evening - Match Experience

Join thousands of devoted fans at Estadio Alejandro Villanueva and experience the electric atmosphere of Peruvian football. After the match, celebrate (or commiserate) with locals at traditional gathering spots.

What makes Alianza Lima special

  • Founded in 1901, one of Peru's oldest and most successful clubs
  • Known as "Los Íntimos" (The Close Ones), reflecting their deep community bonds
  • Historic rivalry with Universitario de Deportes in the "Clásico del fútbol peruano"
  • 23-time Peruvian champions with passionate working-class fanbase
  • Iconic blue and white colors represent Lima's coastal identity

Practical info

  • Lima has a mild climate year-round; bring layers for evening matches
  • Spanish basics are helpful but not required - your guide will translate
  • Match schedules are announced monthly during the season
  • Travel insurance and required documentation are your own responsibility
  • Respect local customs and follow your guide's advice during the experience

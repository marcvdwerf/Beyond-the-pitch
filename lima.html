Is this trip suitable for solo travelers?

Absolutely. Many of our travelers join solo and appreciate the small-group setting. The shared matchday experience makes it easy to connect with like-minded people from the start.

Yes. Matchdays are guided by trusted local hosts who understand the neighborhoods, supporter culture and stadium dynamics. We move as a group, use safe routes and choose moments that allow you to experience the atmosphere without unnecessary risk.

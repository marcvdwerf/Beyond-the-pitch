About Us

Where Sport, Culture and Travel Come Together

Beyond the Pitch is a global travel concept built around a simple belief: to truly understand a place, you need to experience the sports, traditions and communities that shape it.

We create small-scale, authentic travel experiences that go far beyond stadiums and tourist hotspots. Our journeys are designed for people who want to see the world differently — travellers who want to meet the locals, feel the culture, and discover the stories behind the sports that unite communities worldwide.

From Mongolian wrestling to rugby in South Africa, from Rio's football energy to the Highland Games in Scotland, Beyond the Pitch unlocks the real heartbeat of a destination.

Our Promise

Mission

To connect travellers with the true spirit of a destination through sport, culture and community — creating authentic, respectful and memorable experiences led by local partners.

Vision

To become a global platform for cultural sports travel, setting a new standard for meaningful exploration by combining local tradition, athletic passion and human connection.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Authenticity

We work with local communities, athletes, guides and storytellers who represent the real culture of their home — ensuring every experience feels genuine.

Community First

Every destination is built in collaboration with locals. They lead, we support. Their stories shape the journey.

Respect

We approach every culture, tradition and community with integrity and care. Our role is to listen first, then share.

Connection

Sport is our gateway, but people are at the centre of everything we do — travellers, fans, families and local hosts.

Curated Quality

Small groups, well-crafted itineraries, safe execution and professional guidance ensure that every traveller feels supported and inspired.

Beyond the Game

The match is only the starting point. We take travellers deeper — into rituals, food, stories, music and daily life.

Meet the Founders

Founder

Marc van der Werf

Born and based in the Netherlands, I've built my career in tourism, working on global projects for some of the world's largest airlines and leading international ground-transportation initiatives. Along the way, I discovered something meaningful: sport is far more than entertainment — it's a universal language that connects people, cultures, and communities.

With a degree in Sports Marketing and a lifelong passion for exploring new cultures through food, language, and local experiences, I felt inspired to create a platform that brings all these elements together. That idea grew into Beyond the Pitch.

Co-Founder

Martijn Berden

Football has been at the center of my life for as long as I can remember. I played professionally for around ten years, starting in the academy of PSV Eindhoven and later continuing my career across several first- and second-division clubs in the Netherlands. Today, I'm still active at the third level of Dutch football.

After stepping away from the highest professional level, I felt a strong desire to share what football has given me: the energy of performing for a crowd, the joy of entertaining people, and the unique connection that sport creates between players and fans. Combined with my passion for discovering new cultures, Beyond the Pitch gives me the perfect opportunity to bring these worlds together.

Why We Exist

Because sport is more than competition.

It's culture. It's human. It's a universal language.

Through that language, travellers can experience the world in a deeper, more meaningful way.

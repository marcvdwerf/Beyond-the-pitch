Experience Highlights

The Nine White Banners

Watch the sacred procession carry the white stallion tail banners from the Government House to the National Stadium.

Ancestral Sports

Get close-up access to world-class wrestling, archery, and ankle-bone shooting competitions.

Itinerary

Day 1: The Opening Ceremony

Start at 09:00 with the Square parade. At 11:00, witness the Grand Opening Ceremony. For lunch, we eat traditional khuushuur. The rest of the day is filled with wrestling and archery matches.

Day 2: Horse Racing on the Steppe

Depart at 08:00 for the Khui Doloon Khudag valley. Experience the thunderous excitement of child jockeys racing across the steppe. Enjoy a boxed lunch in the open air before returning for the final wrestling rounds.

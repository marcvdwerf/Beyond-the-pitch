This is something deeper

Join Beyond the Pitch for an unforgettable three-day journey to Naadam, Mongolia's most important celebration. This isn't just watching sports - it's experiencing a living tradition that has connected Mongolian people for centuries.

You'll witness breathtaking horse races across the endless steppe, traditional wrestling matches steeped in ritual, and precision archery competitions. More importantly, you'll share meals with nomadic families, learn about their way of life, and understand why Naadam is the heartbeat of Mongolian culture.

Your 3-Day Journey

1

Day 1 - July 10: Zuunmod Local Festival

Experience Naadam like a local

  • Arrival in Ulaanbaatar, meet your group and local guide
  • Travel to Zuunmod (just outside the capital) for a smaller, more intimate Naadam celebration
  • Watch wrestling matches up close, feel the energy of the crowd
  • Evening welcome dinner with traditional Mongolian cuisine
2

Day 2 - July 11: Official National Celebration

The grand spectacle

  • Attend the spectacular opening ceremony at the National Stadium
  • Watch championship wrestling and archery competitions
  • Afternoon visit to a nomadic family in the countryside
  • Learn about their traditions, try airag (fermented mare's milk)
  • Evening: traditional throat singing and cultural performances
3

Day 3 - July 12: The Legendary Horse Races

Into the steppe

  • Travel to the steppe to witness the famous long-distance horse races
  • Watch young jockeys race across 15-30 kilometers of open land
  • Meet trainers and learn about Mongolia's deep connection with horses
  • Farewell lunch celebrating your journey together
  • Return to Ulaanbaatar (departure or extend your stay)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this trip suitable for solo travelers?

Absolutely! Many of our travelers join solo and find it's a great way to connect with like-minded people. You'll be part of a small group throughout the journey.

Do I need a visa for Mongolia?

It depends on your nationality. Many countries (including US, Canada, EU, UK) can visit Mongolia visa-free for up to 30 days. We'll provide specific guidance based on your passport once you register.

What's the accommodation like?

We recommend comfortable 3-4 star hotels in Ulaanbaatar. Accommodation is not included in the package price, giving you flexibility to choose your preferred comfort level. We'll provide specific recommendations.

Can I extend my trip?

Definitely! Mongolia has so much to offer. We can recommend tour operators for extensions to the Gobi Desert, Khuvsgul Lake, or other regions. Many travelers add 3-7 days before or after Naadam.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

Let us know about any dietary requirements when you register. Mongolian cuisine is traditionally meat-heavy, but vegetarian and other options are available, especially in Ulaanbaatar.

How much does it cost?

As this is a pilot journey, we're offering special introductory pricing. Express your interest and we'll send you complete pricing details, payment plans, and what's included.

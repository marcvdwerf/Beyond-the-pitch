Join us at Naadam!

Join Beyond the Pitch for an unforgettable three-day journey to Naadam, Mongolia's most famous festival. Enjoy traditional wrestling matches, breathtaking horse racing, archery, and authentic culture in small travel groups.

When

Mid-July - 3 days (dates vary annually)

Where

Ulaanbaatar & nearby Naadam fields

Included

Guided festival visits, local guides, selected meals, cultural activities

Group size

Small groups for optimal cultural experience

Register your interest See other projects

Sample itinerary: 3 days

Day 1 - Arrival & Opening

Arrival in Ulaanbaatar, transfer to hotel, introduction and attending the Naadam opening ceremony.

Day 2 - Wrestling & Local encounter

In the morning the wrestling matches, in the afternoon visit to a nomadic family, in the evening traditional music and dance.

Day 3 - Horse racing & Farewell dinner

Experience legendary horse races, enjoy performances and a joint farewell dinner.

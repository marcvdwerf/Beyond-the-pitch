Common Questions

How difficult is it to get tickets for the opening ceremony?

Stadium tickets are extremely limited and often sell out instantly. Our local partnerships allow us to secure guaranteed seating for our group as part of the itinerary.

What if I am vegetarian?

Mongolian festival food is meat-heavy, but we can arrange vegetarian boxed lunches for Day 2. Please notify us at least 30 days before the festival.

Is Ulaanbaatar safe during the festival?

Yes, the city is very festive and safe. However, like any large event, crowds can be dense. Your guide will be with you to navigate the busy areas and ensure a smooth experience.

What is the "Nine White Banners" procession?

This is a deeply symbolic ritual. The banners, made from the hair of white stallions, represent the Mongol State. They are carried from the Government House to the stadium to bless the festival.

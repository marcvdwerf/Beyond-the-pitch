Beyond the Pitch × Naadam Festival, Mongolia

Experience three days of wrestling, horse racing, archery and traditional culture in the heart of Mongolia.

Join us at Naadam

Join Beyond the Pitch for an unforgettable three-day journey to Naadam, Mongolia’s most iconic festival. You'll witness traditional Mongolian wrestling, thrilling horse racing, and archery competitions — all while connecting with local communities, enjoying traditional music and sampling regional cuisine.

When

Mid July — 3 days (dates vary yearly)

Where

Ulaanbaatar & nearby Naadam grounds

What's included

Guided festival access, local guides, selected meals, cultural visits

Group size

Small groups to maximize cultural immersion

Sample 3‑Day Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival & Opening Ceremonies

Arrive in Ulaanbaatar, transfer to hotel, orientation and attending the opening ceremonies of Naadam.

Day 2 — Wrestling & Local Visits

Watch the wrestling competitions in the morning, visit a nomadic family in the afternoon and enjoy traditional music and dance in the evening.

Day 3 — Horse Racing & Farewell

Experience the famous horse races, final cultural performances, and farewell dinner.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our journeys.

Who are these journeys for?

Our trips are designed for travelers who want more than a standard tourist experience. Whether you're a solo traveler, a couple, or a group of friends, if you have a passion for sports culture and authentic human connection, you'll feel right at home.

What is included in the price?

While each trip varies, we always include match or event tickets, local expert guides, curated cultural experiences, and internal transport. International flights are generally not included unless specified.

Are the groups small?

Yes. To maintain authenticity and ensure we don't overwhelm the local communities we visit, we keep our groups small-typically between 8 and 14 people.

I am a solo traveler, can I join?

Absolutely! More than half of our community starts their journey solo. It's the fastest way to make new friends who share your specific interests.

Beyond the Pitch √ó Naadam Festival Mongolia

Join us for an unforgettable three-day journey into the heart of Mongolia's most important celebration: the Naadam Festival.

Program

  • July 10: Zuunmod - Experience the local Naadam Festival just outside Ulaanbaatar.
  • July 11: Ulaanbaatar - Be part of the official opening ceremony and watch wrestling and archery at the stadium.
  • July 12: The Steppe - See the famous horse races and meet trainers to learn about nomadic traditions.

About Naadam

Naadam is Mongolia's national holiday, centered on three traditional sports: wrestling, horse racing, and archery. It is a living tribute to the country's nomadic heritage, filled with music, dance, food, and vibrant community spirit.

Why travel with us

Our program gives you access to events that are normally hard for visitors to attend, including the opening ceremony. With guidance from a local expert, you'll experience Naadam not as a tourist, but as part of the celebration.

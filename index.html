Discover the Naadam Festival 2026

Experience Mongolia’s iconic festival with wrestling, horse racing, and archery. Dive deep into the cultural traditions, local ceremonies, and unforgettable performances.

  • Wrestling: Witness ancient Mongolian wrestling matches in traditional costumes.
  • Horse Racing: Follow local riders over long distances across scenic landscapes.
  • Archery: See expert archers demonstrate centuries-old techniques.
  • Local culture: Enjoy music, dance, and food while learning about Mongolian heritage.

About the project

Beyond the pitch delivers immersive, affordable sports and culture travel experiences worldwide.

From rugby in South Africa, to Muay Thai in Thailand, Capoeira in Brazil, Gaelic football in Ireland, and Mongolian wrestling — we explore authentic global sports cultures.

FAQ

Who can apply?

Any club or sports organization seeking global experiences and cultural engagement.

Commitment to storytelling and co-creating fan-centered experiences.

Is there a cost?

The pilot is fully supported; costs are transparently discussed before participation.
